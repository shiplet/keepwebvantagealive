Keep My Webvantage Timer Alive Dammit

To prevent that notorious session timeout, just drag the link below into your bookmarks bar.

Then, after you log in to Webvantage, click the KeepTimerAlive bookmark.

Voila! You'll no longer be logged out every two hours.

P.S. - You'll need to click/activate it every time you refresh, log out, navigate away, etc...

Last tested on Webvantage Aqua v6.70.00.18
